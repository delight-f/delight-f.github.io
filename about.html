About Me

About Me

Dr Faraaz is a specialist anaesthetist committed to delivering safe, high-quality, evidence-based care. He combines modern anaesthetic practice with a patient-centred approach, ensuring every individual receives personalised care and clear communication throughout their surgical journey.

Training & Background

Dr Faraaz graduated from the University of London in 2012, achieving two Distinctions, four Merits, and a Prize for Academic Excellence.

His medical career began in General Practice, where he completed specialist GP training and worked extensively in both the United Kingdom and Australia. This broad clinical experience provided a strong foundation in comprehensive patient care across all stages of life.

He subsequently undertook specialist training in Anaesthesia through the Queensland training program, developing expertise in perioperative medicine, patient safety, and modern anaesthetic techniques.

Qualifications

Dr Faraaz holds Fellowships in both Anaesthesia and General Practice, reflecting his commitment to excellence across multiple areas of medicine.

In addition, he has completed five postgraduate diplomas and holds further qualifications in geriatric medicine, tropical medicine and hygiene, and pre-hospital and retrieval medicine.

Professional Appointments

  • Staff Specialist Anaesthetist — Sunshine Coast University Hospital
  • Specialist General Practitioner — Morayfield Health Hub
  • Senior Lecturer — Griffith University

Philosophy of Care

Dr Faraaz believes exceptional healthcare begins with listening. Drawing on his background in both General Practice and Anaesthesia, he takes a holistic view of each patient's health, recognising that every individual brings unique needs, concerns, and circumstances to their care.

He works closely with patients, surgeons, and healthcare teams to deliver safe, compassionate, and evidence-based anaesthetic care. His focus is on achieving the best possible outcomes while ensuring patients feel informed, supported, and confident throughout their treatment journey.
