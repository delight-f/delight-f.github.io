# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

- **Referring surgeons** — need to verify credentials and find contact information quickly. Primary path: Home → Experience → Contact.
- **Prospective patients** — need reassurance and a clear enquiry path. Primary path: Home → About → Contact.
- **Medical colleagues** — scan background, find academic links, connect on LinkedIn.

## Product Purpose

Establish trust and make contact easy for a specialist anaesthetist serving the Sunshine Coast and regional Queensland. The site's single job: convert a visitor's initial interest into a contact form submission or direct email.

Success looks like: referring surgeons can verify credentials within 3 seconds; patients feel confident reaching out; the site ranks for relevant search terms.

## Brand Personality

**Authoritative · Warm · Precise**

The visual identity sits on the spectrum between clinical precision (anaesthesia, evidence-based medicine) and human warmth (patient care, GP background). Voice is professional but approachable — a consultant speaking directly to colleagues and patients, not institutional copy.

## Anti-references

- Generic medical template sites (stock photos, clip-art caduceus, overused blue/white schemes)
- Single-page scroll pattern (2019 era — this was the previous design)
- AI-template defaults: warm cream background + terracotta accent + serif display
- Overly clinical / cold hospital-style design
- SaaS-style dashboards or app UI patterns

## Design Principles

1. **Multi-page by default** — each page has a distinct URL, purpose, and canonical. No single-page scroll.
2. **Editorial confidence** — content dictates layout, not templates. Thin focused pages beat a long scroll.
3. **Scannability first** — key information readable in under 3 seconds (credentials, contact path).
4. **One memorable moment** — restrained motion (hero photo fade-in, card hover zoom), not scattered effects.
5. **Dark mode as first-class** — not a bolt-on. All surfaces and form inputs adjust.
6. **Frictionless conversion** — contact path has zero unnecessary steps. Form-only contact (no exposed email).

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.2 AA target
- Semantic HTML as the foundation (landmarks, heading hierarchy, form labels)
- Reduced motion media query respected throughout
- Focus-visible styles on all interactive elements
- Skip link on every page
- Form validation with clear error messages
