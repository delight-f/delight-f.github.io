Training & experience

Undergraduate Training

Graduated from the prestigious University of London in 2012 with two Clinical Distinctions, four Merits, and a Prize for Academic Excellence.

Specialist Anaesthesia

Fellow of the Royal Australian College of Anaesthetists and Membership of the Royal College of Anaesthetists (UK).

Queensland-trained in Brisbane, Sunshine Coast and rural placements. Fellowship training in retrieval and perioperative medicine.

Specialist General Practice

Fellowship of the Royal Australian College of General Practitioners and Membership of the Royal College of General Practitioners (UK).

Consultant family physician with busy practices in both the UK and Queensland. Accredited GP anaesthetist.

Prehospital & Retrieval Medicine

Diploma in Prehospital and Retrieval Medicine (Australia) and Diploma in Immediate Medical Care (UK). Queensland Health-accredited specialist.

Tropical Medicine

Diploma in Tropical Medicine and Hygiene, Royal College of Physicians (London). Interest in remote area working, disaster medicine and underserved communities.

Additional Qualifications

Diplomas in Geriatric Medicine (Older adults) and Obstetrics and Gynaecology. All United States licensing examinations complete.
